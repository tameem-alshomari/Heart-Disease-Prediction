Objective
Predict the presence of heart disease in patients.

Key Steps
Data scaling & preprocessing
Medical feature engineering (risk score, stress indicators)
Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, Neural Networks
Threshold tuning (critical for healthcare)

Results
High model performance across multiple algorithms
Optimized for high recall (disease detection)
Successfully identified key medical risk factors

Medical Insight

Heart disease is strongly associated with:

High stress on the heart (oldpeak)
Low maximum heart rate (thalach)
High cholesterol
Age and arterial blockage
